# About Go Planner or Journey Builder or User Journeys

User Journeys (Go Planner) in the gaming industry means automatically guiding players with the right messages and rewards based on how they play the game. Instead of the game company manually contacting players, the system tracks player actions and sends helpful notifications, tips, or offers at the right time. This keeps players interested, improves their experience, and encourages them to keep playing or make in-game purchases.

## For example : 

When a new player installs the game and logs in for the first time, they automatically receive a welcome message explaining the controls and basic gameplay. If the player finishes the tutorial, the system rewards them with bonus coins and suggests trying a multiplayer match. If they lose several matches in a row, the game sends tips on improving aim and survival strategies, along with an option to practice in training mode.

## Example 2 : 

If the player hasn’t opened the game for three days, they receive a push notification saying, “Your squad misses you! Come back and claim a free reward.” When they return, they get a limited-time skin or bonus XP to re-engage them. Later, if the player reaches a high level, the system promotes premium battle passes or special tournaments because advanced players are more likely to spend money.


## Benefits or Advantages :

### Better Player Onboarding : 
New players get guided tutorials, tips, and rewards at the right time. This helps them understand the game faster and reduces confusion.

### Higher Player Engagement
Players keep receiving relevant messages, challenges, and events based on their activity. This keeps the game interesting and prevents boredom.

### Improved Player Retention
If players become inactive, automated reminders and comeback rewards bring them back. This reduces player drop-off.

### Increased In-Game Revenue

Well-timed promotions (battle passes, skins, bundles) are shown to players who are most likely to purchase.
This improves conversion without annoying users.

### Personalized Player Experience

Different players receive different journeys:
    1. Beginners → tutorials
    2. Casual players → fun events
    3. Competitive players → ranked matches
    4. Paying players → premium content
